Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to take the quizzer?

Taking the quizzer is legal. All the materials that are displayed and given out were open-source. We also strictly abide by the rules of fair use.

Is it guaranteed that the questions will appear on the actual CSE Examination?

Sadly no, the quizzer is an assessment which will test your knowledge. It also allows you to gain new knowledge that will prepare you for the actual CSE Examination.

What is the purpose of the survey that appears before taking the quizzer?

One of the purposes of the survey is to determine the ages and occupations that are the most likely to take the CSE Examination.
